# MindBridge

## A Web Platform for Mental Well-being and Community Support

MindBridge is a full-stack web application designed to support mental well-being through self-reflection and community engagement. The platform enables users to track their moods, maintain personal journals, participate in support circles, and manage their profiles in a secure and user-friendly environment.

The goal of MindBridge is to create a digital space where individuals can better understand their emotional patterns, express their thoughts freely, and connect with supportive communities.

---

## Features

### Mood Tracker

* Record daily moods through an intuitive interface.
* Maintain a history of emotional trends.
* Visualize mood patterns over time.
* Encourage self-awareness and emotional monitoring.

### Personal Journal

* Create and save journal entries.
* Maintain a private collection of reflections and thoughts.
* Easily access previously saved entries.
* Promote consistent self-expression and mindfulness.

### Support Circles

* Join communities based on shared interests and experiences.
* Engage with supportive discussions.
* Discover and participate in relevant circles.
* Foster meaningful peer-to-peer interactions.

### User Profile

* Manage personal information and preferences.
* Customize user experience.
* Maintain a personalized dashboard.

## Workflow

1. User creates an account or logs in.
2. Accesses a personalized dashboard.
3. Records moods through the Mood Tracker.
4. Writes and saves journal entries.
5. Joins and interacts with Support Circles.
6. Manages profile information and preferences.
7. Continues tracking emotional well-being over time.
